Tricky‚Äëarea tips

  • Debt basis requires direct loans by the shareholder (not mere guarantees unless back‚Äëto‚Äëback documented).
  • Ordering matters: Increase basis for income before distributions, then reduce for distributions/nondeductibles, then losses.
  • Pro‚Äërata rule: When basis is insufficient for multiple loss items, allocate allowed amounts proportionally across all losses/deductions for the year.

  • BIG vs PII: BIG applies during the recognition period; PII tax needs AEP and PII >25% of receipts.
